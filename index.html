ABOUT US

Nyamata High school is a private school found in Bugesera district, it’s both day and boarding school.Our school’s performance is very good because in the last 26 years our students are among the best students who get scholarships abroad and make big carriers in different aspects.

MISION & VISION

Mission: To be a center of skills and quality education whose students shall be role models and transform society. Vision: To create a competent, innovative and creative students with best skills to serve Rwanda efficiently.
